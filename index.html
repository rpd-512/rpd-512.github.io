Publications
On Universality and Efficient Arithmetic Realisation of Ternary Logic Gates
Rhiddhi Prasad Das, Jyotiprakash Mishra
Exhaustively evaluated all 19,683 binary-arity ternary gates; confirmed 3,774 are functionally complete. Proved unary completeness implies full completeness via Rosenberg's theorem (10,529× speedup). Classified gates into 322 isomorphism classes under S₃×Z₂ (47,105× speedup). Best ternary ripple-carry adder achieves 18% lower propagation depth and 9.4% fewer gates than binary NAND at 32-bit width.
Preprint · Zenodo · Mar 2026 Ternary Logic Computer Architecture
Inverse Design of Patch Antenna Geometry based on S11 using Nearest Refinement Algorithm
Rhiddhi Prasad Das, Vishwajeet Sharan
Proposed NRA, an adaptive refinement-based inverse design method achieving up to 55% spectral MSE reduction while preserving physically valid antenna geometries.
